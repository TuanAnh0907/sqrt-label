Krzysztof Topolski

PRESENCE

Krzysztof Topolski : field recordings, composition, production

In the Field : Suwalki Region is a very sentimental project. I was born in Kalinowo, but I spent my childhood and youth in Suwalki. The project is based on a series of field recordings made in the Suwalki Region. Wandering through forests and fields and looking for the sound, I return home, I touch the past and explore my memory. I return to places I know and discover new ones.

All sounds recorded in Krzywe, Wigry, Sejny, Suwalki, Skajsgiry, Kiekskiejmy, Dowspuda, Rospuda-Mlynisko, Jalowo-Kurianki, Plociczno and Gdansk in 2013.

Part of the field recordings is presented in form of a collection of sound notes on the audio-blog.

Very special thanks to Monika Pasiecznik, Michal Libera, Krzysztof Miekus, Marek Galazka, Szymon Bukowski, Thomas Wells and all my family.

Realized as part of the Minister of Culture and National Heritage scholarship.
